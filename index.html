Biography

Ben Laufer is a postdoctoral fellow in the LaSalle lab at the University of California Davis. He makes us of computational and experimental methods to examine the epigenetic interface between neurodevelopment and environment. When not working away on his research, he contributes to EpiGenie as the Epigenetics Editor. Outside of the science world he enjoys biking, swimming, and photography.
